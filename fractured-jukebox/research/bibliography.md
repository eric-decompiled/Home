## Citation Notes

When citing these works:

1. **Highly-cited foundational works** should be cited when establishing theoretical grounding
2. **Survey papers** (Lima 2021, Khulusi 2020) are excellent for literature review sections
3. **ISMIR papers** are the standard for MIR technical contributions
4. **PMC papers** are freely accessible and can be linked directly

For music visualization projects specifically, the most relevant papers are:
- Lima et al. (2021) for technique survey
- Palmer et al. (2013) for color-music emotional mapping
- Sapp (2001) for harmonic visualization
- Koelsch (2014) for brain-emotion grounding
